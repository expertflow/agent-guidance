How to login to my URA Web Portal Account?

Follow these steps to login

  • 1. Visit URA portal
  • 2. Click on the Login area on the top right corner
  • 3. Enter username (TIN) and password

NB. If you are logging in for the very first time, you will enter the system default password which was sent to your registered email when your TIN application was approved. There after you will be required to create your personal password.

For further assistance, please contact us on 0800117000/0800217000 (toll free)
